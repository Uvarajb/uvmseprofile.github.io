Module 8 - RMPP

Research Methods and Professional Practice

Introudction:

Welcome to the Research Methods and Professional Practice module, a crucial step in my academic journey that will shape and improve my research skills. The University of Essex has designed this module to provide students with a practical foundation in research methods, empowering us to confidently navigate research endeavors in real-world settings. We will immerse ourselves in a variety of essential tools and techniques for any researcher, with the opportunity for continuous feedback through carefully selected case studies and project tasks. In addition to equipping us with practical research skills, this module is meticulously designed to offer essential insights into the legal, social, ethical, and professional dimensions of computing projects. Understanding and adhering to the relevant codes of practice and professional standards is crucial, and this module ensures that we have a solid foundation of knowledge and skills in these areas.

The learning materials, including two core textbooks by Dawson (2015) and Bott (2014), provide a comprehensive overview of the subject. These resources ground us in the scientific method and offer detailed guidance on conducting research across various types of organizations. As I engage with these texts and the module content, I have been challenged to integrate the practicalities of research with critical analysis of key principles of research methodology. This synthesis of knowledge has drawn from the various modules of my program, culminating in a holistic understanding of research and professional practice. This module is not just a learning experience; it is a journey of reflection, application, and transformation.
